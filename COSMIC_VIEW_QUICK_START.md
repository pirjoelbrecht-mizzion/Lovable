## How to Use

### Viewing Your Week

1. Navigate to the **Quest** page
2. The Cosmic View loads by default
3. Each vertical line represents a day (Mon-Sun)
4. Bubbles along each line are your workouts for that day
5. The weekly stress indicator at the top shows training load

### Understanding the Display

**Day Labels**: Circular badges at the top
- Regular days: Purple circle with letter
- Today: Cyan circle with glow effect

**Workout Bubbles**:
- Size indicates importance (bigger = more important)
- Color shows workout type
- Outer ring slowly rotates
- Today's workout pulses continuously

**Connection Lines**:
- Show progression through the week
- Connect workouts across days
- Animated gradient effect

### Interacting with Workouts

**To view details**:
1. Hover over a bubble
2. Tooltip appears with workout info
3. Click to open full details modal

**To add a workout**:
1. Find a day with no workouts (shows "+" button)
2. Click the "+" button
3. Add your custom workout

**To complete a workout**:
1. Click on today's workout
2. View workout details
3. Mark as complete (from detail modal)
4. Bubble turns green with checkmark

## Tips & Tricks

### Best Practices
1. **Keep it organized**: Add all workouts for the week to see the full picture
2. **Use workout types**: Proper type classification ensures correct colors
3. **Check daily**: Today's workout is always highlighted
4. **Review connections**: Lines show training flow through the week

### What to Look For
- **Heavy days**: Multiple bubbles = high training load
- **Recovery spacing**: Green/light green bubbles between hard sessions
- **Progressive build**: Gradual increase in workout intensity
- **Balance**: Mix of workout types throughout the week

### Common Patterns

**Typical Week**:
```
Mon: Recovery run (light green)
Tue: Intervals (red)
Wed: Easy run (cyan)
Thu: Tempo run (purple)
```
